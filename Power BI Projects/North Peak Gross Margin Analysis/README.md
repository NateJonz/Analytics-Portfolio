# NorthPeak Business Solutions: Business Analysis Project


## Overview
This project analyzes the operations and sales performance of **NorthPeak Business Solutions**, a multi-state B2B distributor of office equipment and accessories. Despite rapid revenue growth from **$6.1M in 2023 to $202M in 2025**, the company faced declining gross and net income margins. Leadership requested a data-driven assessment to identify potential causes of margin erosion and support strategic decision-making.

The analysis leverages sales, customer, inventory, and shipment data to uncover patterns by **production region**, **product tier**, and **individual products**.

---

## Tools & Techniques
- **Power BI Desktop** – Data modeling, relationships between multiple tables, interactive visualizations
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Custom measures for insights  
- **Microsoft Excel** – Original dataset 
- **PowerPoint** – Strategic recommendations and presentation of insights  

---

## Project Deliverables
1. **Power BI Visualizations**  
   - Interactive visuals breaking down sales and margins by region, product tier, and product  
   - Custom DAX measures to calculate KPIs such as gross margin, net margin, and revenue trends  
   - Drill-through and slicers for dynamic data exploration  

2. **Strategic Recommendations Presentation (PowerPoint)**  
   - Key findings summarized for leadership  
   - Actionable insights to improve gross margin % 

3. **Original Dataset (Excel)**  
   - Includes all sales, customer, and inventory data used for analysis
